⭐ MAP 1 — The Meadows (Tutorial)

Theme: Peaceful lands disturbed.
Plot:
Goblins stole the Life Crystal Shard, weakening the world’s natural magic.
The meadow animals have fled, plants are wilting.

Ariana instructs Glitter (via magical telepathy/the voice in her head).
Glitter begins the adventure.

Goal: Recover the Life Shard fragment.

Tease: Goblins whisper about “The Master” — a shadowy hooded figure.

⭐ MAP 2 — Farmer Bragg’s Field

Theme: Farmland being robbed.
Plot:
The goblins are stealing food and using stolen crops to fuel crude magical bombs.
A fragment of the Life Crystal is embedded in a corrupted scarecrow boss.

Goal: Purify the corrupted scarecrow and retrieve shard #2.

Reveal:
The goblins here mention “the Fire General” — a flaming goblin champion in Map 4.

⭐ MAP 3 — Golden Drylands

Theme: Dying land, seasonal magic disrupted.
Plot:
Without the Life Crystal stabilizing things, the sun’s magic goes wild.
Drylands overheat, goblins mutate into desert variants.

A desert elite goblin wields the third Life Shard.

Goal: Beat the elite, restore region stability.

Reveal:
Goblins mention a gate opening “deep in the Fire Realm.”

⭐ MAP 4 — Ember Realm (Fire Castle)

Theme: Fire chaos, lava, corruption.
Plot:
The Fire Crystal has been cracked.
A Fire Goblin General leads an army trying to fully corrupt it.

You’re not just recovering a shard —
The whole Fire Crystal is at risk.

Goal:
Defeat Fire General, purify the Fire Crystal.

Reveal:
The Fire General serves the Shadow Architect, a Void Entity manipulating goblins.

⭐ MAP 5 — Frost Kingdom (Ice Castle)

Theme: Frozen palace, ice magic spiraling out of control.
Plot:
Because the Fire Realm destabilized, elemental balance is broken.
Ice Realm is freezing uncontrollably.

The Ice Crystal starts pulling mana from everything nearby.

Goal:
Shut down the crystal overload and defeat the Ice Warden corrupted by Void influence.

Reveal:
Ariana senses the Void Crystal awakening.

⭐ MAP 6 — Realm of Light (Holy Sanctuary)

Theme: Blindingly bright, heavenly battlefield.
Plot:
Light Realm tries to expel the corruption —
but this creates explosive bursts of holy power harming the land.

Shadow goblins invade despite literally hating the light.

Goal:
Stop the chain-reaction meltdown and secure the Light Crystal.

Reveal:
The Shadow Architect wants to merge all crystals into one to distort reality.

⭐ MAP 7 — Mushroom Realm (Chaos Zone)

Theme: Wild magic zone mutated by imbalance.
Plot:
With elemental energies fighting each other, a “wild magic bloom” erupted.
Mushrooms become magical, animals get weird, goblins act… bizarre.

This is not a normal realm — it’s a side-effect of breaking the balance.

Goal:
Stabilize the bloom by dispersing corrupted spores contaminating mana flow.

Reveal:
A portal to the Void Realm is nearly open.

⭐ MAP 8 — Void / Gravity Realm (Shadow Gate)

Theme: Floating rocks, anti-gravity, warped space.
Plot:
This is the heart of the corruption.
The Shadow Architect — a Void Entity — wants to reshape the world:

unmake gravity

distort time

rebuild the Crystal Keep as a void fortress

You confront his elite goblins and Void Champions.

Goal:
Defeat the Architect’s avatar…
…but he retreats into the Crystal Keep itself.

⭐ MAP 9 — Crystal Keep Interior (Finale)

Theme: Glitter returns home to find it invaded.
Plot:
The Architect reached the Keep and tries to fuse all five crystals to remake the kingdom in Void image.

Goblins swarm the halls.
The throne room fractures.
Ariana is weakened trying to keep the crystals apart.

Final Battle:
Glitter defeats the Shadow Architect at the heart of the Keep.

Ending:
The Crystals restore balance.
Ariana declares Glitter a Master Guardian of the Crystal Keep.
Kids get a heartwarming finale:

“You did it Glitter… you protected everyone.”

Credits roll.
